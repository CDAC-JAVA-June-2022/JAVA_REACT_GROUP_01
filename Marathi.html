New Marathi Movies

Tanhaji

Tanhaji, a Maratha warrior, is Shivaji Maharaj's trusted lieutenant. When the Mughals invade and conquer Kondhana fort, he sets out to reclaim it for his king and country.

Pawankhind

In 1660, on a dreadful night, Shivaji finds himself cornered in the Panhala fort by the Bijapur Sultanate.

Hambirrao

Based on the Maratha warrior Hansaji Mohite who was later given the title of Sarnobat Hambirrao, the film portrays his life as the Commander in Chief of Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's army.

Pension

Here's a family of three Balu, his mother, and grandmother. They survive on his grandfather's pension of rupees eight hundred a month. Find how this family struggles to survive against odds.

Basta

The families of a bride and groom go above and beyond to find the perfect wedding gift for each other, as it will reflect their financial capabilities and taste.



Popular Marathi Movies

Jatra

In a religious fair, two individuals from the villages Hyalagaad and Tyalagaad, run a race and even kill for winning the hosting rights of the fair.

Duniyadari

Shreyas, a young man with a bitter relationship with his parents, fills the void in his life with loyal friends from his college. However, he struggles to fix his love life by trying to woo Shirin.

Timepass

Dagdu, the son of an autorickshaw driver, falls in love with a girl named Prajakta. However, her parents oppose their relationship and leave the town with her secretly.

Natsamraat

After retirement, Ganpat Belwalkar, a Shakespearean actor, divides his property amongst his two children.

Deool

Keshya, a young man, gets a vision of Lord Dattatreya. He announces it to the village, which garners the attention of politicians and gives rise to a media circus.
